Synthesia

educational VR musical puzzle game

In youth, Luxi studied violin for 8 years, but found progress difficult as she was unable to hear subtle differences between tones. Recently, she felt inspired by the human sensory phenomenon synesthesia to create a game which aims to train the ears of visual learners like herself.
Whenever a player hears a note, a corresponding colour ripples in front of their eyes, simulating synesthesia. This creates and reinforces an audio-visual link, making it easier for music aficionados to differentiate between notes.
